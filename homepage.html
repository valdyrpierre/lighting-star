Welcome to Lightning Stars FC! ⚽✨

Founded in summer 2020, Lightning Stars FC is not just a soccer team; we’re a community of passionate players, dedicated coaches, and supportive fans striving for excellence both on and off the field.

Our Mission: At Lightning Stars FC, our mission is to inspire young athletes through teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. We believe in the power of sports to build character, forge lasting friendships, and create lifelong memories.

Core Values:

  • Teamwork: We play for each other and support one another in every match.
  • Integrity: Fair play and respect are at the heart of our game.
  • Community: We are committed to giving back and being active members of our local community.
